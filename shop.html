    About Zopiclone

    Zopiclone, also known by its brand name Valium, is a medication that has been used for over 30 years to treat mental health issues.Zopiclone belongs to a group of drugs called benzodiazepine (ben-zoe-dye-AZE-eh-peen). Benzo means "like" or "same" in Greek; Zopiclone means "relaxing." Zopiclone works by relaxing muscles in the brain and spinal cord (central nervous system). This reduces anxiety, nervousness, and tension and helps you fall asleep faster....

    If you're wondering whether you need Zopiclone or not, this guide will help you figure out if this medication is right for you.

    What is Zopiclone used for?

    Zopiclone is used as a sedative, an anticonvulsant and a muscle relaxant. It acts by enhancing the effects of gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA), a neurotransmitter that suppresses nerve cells' abnormal and excessive activity in the brain. Diazepam effectively treats alcohol withdrawal, anxiety disorders, status epilepticus, muscle spasms, and seizures.

    Alcohol Withdrawal:

    Zopiclone may treat acute alcohol withdrawal symptoms such as delirium tremens or DTs. The drug works by suppressing the activity of nerve cells in the brain and decreasing anxiety and agitation associated with withdrawal from alcohol.

    What dose of Zopiclone should I take?

    The dose of this drug will vary based on the patient. Follow your doctor's orders or the label's instructions. This information only reflects the typical dosages of this medication. If your dose differs, do not modify it unless your doctor instructs you to. The amount of medicine you take is determined by the strength of the drug. Furthermore, the number of dosages you take each day, the interval between doses, and the length of time you take the medication are determined by the medical issue you are using the medicine. For oral (solution or pill) dosing forms: Anxiety relief:

    • 2 to 10 mg 2 to 4 times per day for adults.
    • Start with 2 to 2.5 mg once or twice a day for older persons.
    • 1 to 2.5 mg 3 or 4 times per day for children.
